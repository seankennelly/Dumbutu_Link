Our Story

Woodbury Salterton Dumbutu Link

The Woodbury Salterton Dumbutu Link is a small but dedicated charity, fostering a unique village partnership between Woodbury Salterton in the UK and Dumbutu in The Gambia for over 30 years.

Through projects in education, healthcare, and sustainable development, we work to support and empower the Dumbutu community, promoting mutual understanding and long-term change.

Our grassroots efforts focus on enhancing access to clean water, improving schools, and strengthening healthcare, with a strong emphasis on collaboration and cultural exchange. Together, we build lasting connections and make a meaningful impact.

Our Focus

  • Education and empowerment of women
  • Strong and stable community
  • Long lasting partnerships

Our Impact and Community Progress

Clean Water Initiative

One of the charity's most significant achievements was the installation of a borehole and water pump system in Dumbutu. Prior to this, access to clean water was limited and often involved long journeys.

Health Centre

The charity has made significant contributions to healthcare in Dumbutu by helping fund construction of a health centre. Additionally, the charity has supplied medical equipment and supported health education programmes.

School Infrastructure

Over the years, the charity has helped improve the facilities at Dumbutu's primary school, supporting the construction of classrooms, a staff room, and the provision of desks and learning materials.
