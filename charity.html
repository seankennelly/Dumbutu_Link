The Link

The Woodbury Salterton Dumbutu Link is a small, community-driven charity that has been fostering a unique village link between Woodbury Salterton in Devon, UK, and Dumbutu in The Gambia for over 30 years. The charity was founded with the aim of promoting friendship, understanding, and mutual support between the two communities, focusing on improving education, healthcare, and sustainable development in Dumbutu while also enriching lives in Woodbury Salterton through cultural exchange and learning.

A Friendship Across Continents

At the heart of the charity's work is the long-standing relationship between the two villages. This connection has grown from a simple exchange of ideas and goodwill into a meaningful partnership that has transformed lives in both communities. Over the years, residents of Woodbury Salterton have visited Dumbutu, and in turn, representatives from Dumbutu have been welcomed to the UK. These exchanges allow both villages to learn from each other, bridging cultural gaps and celebrating shared humanity.

Supporting Education and Development

One of the charity's core missions is to support educational initiatives in Dumbutu. The village's primary school has been a focal point for the charity's efforts, with funding going towards the provision of school supplies, teacher support, and the construction of essential infrastructure like classrooms and sanitation facilities. By helping to improve access to education, the charity aims to empower children in Dumbutu with the knowledge and skills they need to shape their future. In addition to education, the charity has been involved in several development projects aimed at improving healthcare, access to clean water, and agricultural sustainability in Dumbutu. These initiatives are designed to be community-led, ensuring that the people of Dumbutu have a strong voice in their own development.

Our projects are led by the needs and voices of the Dumbutu community, ensuring that development is sustainable and culturally appropriate

A Sustainable Approach

The Woodbury Salterton Dumbutu Link is committed to sustainability in all of its projects. Rather than imposing external solutions, the charity works closely with the people of Dumbutu to identify their needs and develop solutions that are practical, culturally appropriate, and environmentally sustainable. This collaborative approach has allowed the charity to create long-lasting impact while ensuring that the community retains ownership of its development journey.

A Two-Way Exchange

While much of the charity's work is focused on supporting Dumbutu, the relationship is very much a two-way exchange. The people of Woodbury Salterton have gained a deeper understanding of Gambian culture and traditions through visits, school programs, and community events that celebrate the link. The charity actively works to promote cross-cultural learning, helping to broaden horizons and deepen connections on both sides.

Looking Toward the Future

As the charity looks to the future, its mission remains clear: to continue nurturing the bond between Woodbury Salterton and Dumbutu, supporting sustainable development in the village while creating opportunities for both communities to learn from and inspire each other. The Woodbury Salterton Dumbutu Link remains a testament to the power of grassroots charity work, proving that even small actions can have a big impact when rooted in friendship and mutual respect.

This partnership has shown us that even small communities, working together with respect and compassion, can make a big difference in the world.
